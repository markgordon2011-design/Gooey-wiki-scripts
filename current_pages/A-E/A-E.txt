'''Abyssal Realms''' (u•bis•sul): The Abyssal Realms are the planar dominions of the gods aligned with Evil. These realms are the source of demonic, fiendish, and corrupted entities that influence mortals through pacts, curses, and dark magic​. 

'''Aethyr''' (ā•thēr):A nomadic race known as Windweavers, the Aethyr have an intrinsic connection to air and the sky. They are tall, with pale, greyish-purple skin and an elegant, almost ethereal grace. Their culture revolves around travel, trade, and honor-bound societal structures, shaped by their deific origins​. 

'''Aeverlund Estate''': Hrumm Manor

'''Aduurn River''': A vital waterway in West Verdestia that flows from the Wyrmrest Mountains, joining the Mystrune River near Trundadelve. The river is crucial for trade and defense, with dwarven-engineered locks allowing transport through its otherwise treacherous waters​.

'''Af ’morn''': A time designation referring to the hours between nine bells and twelve bells, marking the transition from morning to midday​. 

'''Af ’noons''': The time after Highsul but before dusk, roughly equivalent to afternoon​.

'''Agents of Aendun’Azuu''': A clandestine organization deeply tied to the history of the Dark Elves. Their activities and influence remain shrouded in secrecy​.

'''Algidian''' (al•ji•dē•en): A collective term referring to reptilian races such as the Gamithians and Lizardfolk. They are known for their resilience, cold-blooded nature, and varied cultural traditions​. 

'''Ablume''' (u•bloom): The northern polar continent of Zyathé, known for its extreme cold, barren landscapes, and hidden horrors. The Bloodpine Forest in the south of Ablume is infamous for its abyssal corruption and lurking fiends​. 

'''Ambershue''': A Fay’aree court located in the Feywyrd Forest, south-east of the Wyrmrest Mountains. It is renowned for its sprawling apple orchards and mystical influence​.

'''Amysthal''' (am•iss•thol): A once-great floating Ethernic metropolis that now hangs lifeless in the sky, filled with horrific abominations. It was nearly destroyed during the Woe of Ruin and remains a place of great danger​. 

'''Anarchria''' (an•or•crē•u): Known as the ''Havoc Dealer'', Anarchria is the lord of chaos and the leader of the Four Lords of Corruption. His influence spreads entropy and destruction across the world​. 

'''Andvalla''' (and•vo•lu): A wealthy and powerful sovereignty located south of the Republic of Zyranthea. Known for its long history, strong naval presence, and influential aristocracy​.
