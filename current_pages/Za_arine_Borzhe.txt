=== '''Za'arine Borzhe (Zyathé)''' ===
'''Overview'''

Za’arine Borzhe is a well-known and influential captain in Zyathé’s maritime circles, respected for her skill at sea and sharp instincts in trade and privateering., serving as the captain of ''Durak’s Luck'', a well-known Estrenian junk out of Darkenhaven. She is an active member of the '''Council of Sails''', holding one of the prestigious Nine of Sails seats, which grants her influence over privateering and trade across the region. While she claims to be just a common sailor with extraordinary luck, those who interact with her note that she is unusually well-spoken and adept at navigating both the rough docks of Gloomport and the refined halls of Darkenhaven’s elite.

'''Physical Description'''

Za’arine presents herself with the air of a seasoned sailor, often seen in a well-fitted navy coat with gold trim, a ruffled shirt, and a tricorn hat adorned with blue and white feathers. Her defining features include a tattooed neckline and a distinctive facial scar. She wears multiple silver and turquoise medallions, hinting at connections to maritime traditions or superstitions. Her confident stance and keen gaze suggest both authority and charisma.

'''Personality'''

Za’arine is a charismatic and adaptable leader, capable of switching seamlessly between the crude vernacular of sailors and the refined speech of merchants and nobility. She is known for her sharp wit, pragmatic decision-making, and fierce loyalty to her crew. Her ability to maintain alliances with figures such as '''Commodore Oakfire''' and both Thessens suggests a deep understanding of political and economic maneuvering.

'''Abilities or Special Qualities'''

* '''Master Navigator''' – Za’arine possesses an unmatched ability to chart dangerous waters, evade pursuit, and find hidden routes.
* '''Silver-Tongued''' – She can effortlessly transition between different social circles, making her an effective negotiator.
* '''Privateer’s Cunning''' – Her ship, ''Durak’s Luck'', is part of both Darkenhaven’s merchant fleet and its privateer navy, giving her legal cover for more illicit activities.

'''Background / History'''

Though Za’arine has long made Gloomport and Darkenhaven her home base, she is not originally from Zyranthea. Her past remains largely unknown, as she deflects personal inquiries with humor or misdirection. What is clear is that she has spent years cultivating a successful career as a privateer and merchant, securing lucrative contracts and building a reputation as a reliable—if unpredictable—player in the seafaring world.

'''Alliances & Relationships'''

* '''Council of Sails''' – The Council is a major governing body for ships in Gloomport, rotating leadership among captains​.
* '''Lady Aramitha Fontaine''' – Maintains a standing contract with Fontaine’s Findings for rare and exotic goods.

'''Notable Appearances or Involvement'''

Za’arine and ''Durak’s Luck'' are well known in the waters around Darkenhaven, often seen conducting trade or patrolling as privateers. Her dealings with high-ranking merchants and military officials suggest deeper involvement in regional politics and commerce.
