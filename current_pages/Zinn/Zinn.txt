
== Description ==
This unexplored continent is covered in thick impenetrable jungles<ref>The Cyclopaedia Volume 1: The Wy'rded World, p. 86</ref>.  Reports of an enormous pyramid that can be seen from the shores. 

== Towns/Cities ==
Small resupply villages on the beaches struggle to survive.
