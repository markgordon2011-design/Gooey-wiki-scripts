
=== '''Alphinius Goo (Zyathé)''' ===

==== '''Overview''' ====
Alphinius Goo is a renowned wizard, scholar, and an eccentric figure in the world of '''Zyathé'''. Best known for his mastery of '''oozes, slimes, and gelatinous creatures''', he has developed numerous bizarre yet potent spells and techniques. He serves as an '''emeritus representative of Zyranthea''' and is a well-regarded, if somewhat mischievous, magical figure.

==== '''Physical Description''' ====
Alphinius Goo is an older, '''portly male human''' with an infectious '''grin and twinkling blue eyes''' that reflect his love for mischief. His '''gray hair and beard''' are meticulously kept, likely to avoid catching fire from his ever-present '''pipe'''. He prefers '''modest but finely crafted attire''', notably '''eschewing traditional wizard robes and pointed hats''' in favor of more practical clothing. '''Also, he often carries an ever full flagon of restorative adult beverage that he sips from when necessary.'''

==== '''Personality''' ====
A '''charismatic and eccentric''' wizard, Alphinius is both beloved and frustrating in equal measure. He is '''enthusiastic, jovial, and quick-witted''', known for his signature '''"HaHA!!"''' exclamation. Though often underestimated due to his playful demeanor, he is an '''intelligent and capable spellcaster''' whose innovations have '''baffled and impressed''' even the most seasoned of mages.

==== '''Abilities & Special Qualities''' ====
Alphinius is particularly famous for his '''unique magical creations involving goo, slime, and gelatinous substances'''. His spells, such as '''Alphinius' Gooey Finger, Slimy Flow, and Torrent of Goo''', showcase his '''expertise in transmutation and conjuration magic''', often creating chaotic yet effective battlefield conditions. While some wizards dismiss his work as frivolous, '''many have learned the hard way not to underestimate the tactical value of his creations'''.

==== '''Background / History''' ====
Alphinius has spent much of his life developing '''innovative spells''', experimenting with oozes, and '''teaching aspiring wizards'''. His fascination with gelatinous creatures has led him to '''unearth lost magical knowledge''', crafting spells that defy conventional arcane wisdom. Over the years, he has gathered '''a loyal circle of scholars, adventurers, and students''', some of whom have adopted his unorthodox approach to magic.

==== '''Alliances & Relationships''' ====

* '''Di’yannea Goo''' – His wife, a highly respected wizard and former '''Dean of Arcane Studies at Darkenhaven University'''. She often helps '''resolve the unintended consequences of Alphinius' magical escapades'''.
* '''Lucasz Gyzaengaxx''' - A firm friend and ally for many years, though '''several of their adventures''' are not safe for the public ear. 
* '''The Tower of Goo''' – A faction or research group centered around '''his unique arcane philosophies and creations'''.
* '''Zyranthean Republic''' – As an '''emeritus representative''', Alphinius holds '''some political influence''', though his unorthodox methods often confound bureaucrats.
* '''Gooey Who? -''' A newspaper and monthly magazine covering '''celebrities and other notable individuals'''. Mischievious but always honest... though certain details do sometimes have to be left out to safeguard individuals or even Zyathé itself from '''unnecessary consequences.''' 
* '''Lord Es'Car'Goo''' - This HIGHLY intelligent Snail, though a fierce competitor to Alphinius in the political arena, was a good friend and secret consultant to him in the arcane world.  They often compared notes on the study of Oozes, Slimes and Gelatinous creatures as his Lordship had insights into the underdark that were important to the work that Alphinius was doing.
